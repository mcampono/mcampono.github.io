Michael Camponovo

I am a GIS educator, outreach coordinator, community-engaged geospatial practitioner, and mentor at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville.

My work connects geospatial technology with teaching, professional development, community projects, student success, and public-facing communication. I develop applied GIS and UAV learning experiences, support geospatial outreach, collaborate on community mapping projects, and mentor students as they build technical skills and professional pathways.
